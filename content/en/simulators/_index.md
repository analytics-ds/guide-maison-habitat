## Four tools to prepare your project

Buying, selling or financing a property requires anticipating the right numbers. Our simulators are **free, instant and require no registration**. They are based on the rules in force in France in 2026 (HCSF, PTZ ceilings, notary fees scales).

- **Borrowing capacity**: calculate the maximum amount you can borrow based on your income and charges, within the legal 35 % debt limit.
- **Loan monthly payments**: estimate your monthly payments and the total cost of the credit (insurance included) for a given amount, duration and rate.
- **Notary fees**: quantify the acquisition costs by property type (old or new), to factor these costs into your global budget.
- **Zero-rate loan (PTZ)**: estimate the amount you can claim depending on your zone, household composition and property type.
