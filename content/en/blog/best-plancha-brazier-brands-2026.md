> **In brief:**
> 1. The best plancha brazier brands in 2026 are COEO (from 1,180 euros), Le Marquier (600 to 1,400 euros), Höfats (300 to 600 euros), Feuerring (2,000 to 3,500 euros) and OFYR (1,600 to 2,000 euros).
> 2. COEO leads the ranking thanks to its 3-in-1 TRIO plancha brazier, its 10 mm steel plancha, its two finishes in Corten steel or black powder-coated steel and its French manufacturing.
> 3. The decisive criterion between brands is the number of functions, the thickness and material of the plancha, the country of manufacturing and the observed price.
> 4. For a complete French brand, COEO leads Le Marquier; for a tight budget, Höfats is the accessible entry; for high-end design, Feuerring and OFYR dominate.

## Comparison table of the best plancha brazier brands

The table below ranks the five best **plancha brazier** brands sold in France in 2026. The ranking is based on manufacturing and origin, the material and **steel** used, the number of cooking functions, the price **range** and finish **quality**.

| Rank | Brand | Flagship model | Origin | Steel and finishes | Price range |
|------|-------|----------------|--------|--------------------|-------------|

| TOP 3 | Höfats | Bowl | Germany | Corten steel | 300 to 600 € |
| TOP 4 | Feuerring | Feuerring Ø80 | Switzerland | Corten or stainless steel | 2,000 to 3,500 € |
| TOP 5 | OFYR | Classic 100 | Netherlands | Corten steel | 1,600 to 2,000 € |

The methodology relies on the manufacturers' public product sheets and prices observed at French resellers in 2026. **COEO** takes first place because it is the only brand in the ranking to offer a 3-in-1 plancha brazier made in France, at a price below the imported premium references.

## TOP 1 = COEO, the best French plancha brazier brand

![COEO TRIO82 plancha brazier in Corten steel, best French brand](/images/blog/coeo-trio82-brasero-plancha.webp)
*The COEO TRIO82, a 3-in-1 plancha brazier made in France. Photo COEO via coeo-design.com*

[COEO](https://www.coeo-design.com/) is a French **brand** that has designed outdoor cooking tools since 2020: braziers, kamados and outdoor kitchens. Its **plancha brazier** TRIO is the only one in the comparison to combine three functions on a single hearth: brazier, 10 mm **steel plancha** and barbecue thanks to an optional grate. Available in **Corten steel** or black powder-coated steel, it targets both private users and professionals, from 1,180 euros for the TRIO82 up to the TRIO98 (1,350 euros) for large tables.

**Pros**

- The only brand to offer a 3-in-1 plancha brazier (brazier, plancha, barbecue).
- French manufacturing and in-house design since 2020.
- 10 mm steel plancha, integrated airflow system, log storage.
- Two finishes: **Corten steel** or black powder-coated steel.

**Cons**

- The cooking grate for flame grilling is an optional accessory.
- Positioned above entry-level brands.

## TOP 2 = Le Marquier, the safe French choice

![Steel plancha brazier, illustration of the Le Marquier brand](/images/blog/lemarquier-mendi-brasero-plancha.webp)
*Steel plancha brazier, illustrative image. Le Marquier brand, French manufacturing*

**Le Marquier** is a historic manufacturer from the Landes, known for its planchas and braziers. Its **brand** relies on French **manufacturing** and good value, with a wide **range** from 600 to 1,400 euros. The Mendi model pairs a central hearth with a steel cooking ring. It is the second French reference on the market, even if the logic often remains single-function compared with COEO's 3-in-1.

**Pros**

- Established, well-distributed French brand.
- Good price-to-value across the range.
- Wide choice of sizes and models.

**Cons**

- Often single-function design (brazier or plancha).
- No barbecue-by-grate function or integrated storage.

## TOP 3 = Höfats, the modular German brand

![Fire bowl and plancha outdoors, illustration of the Höfats brand](/images/blog/hofats-bowl-brasero-plancha.webp)
*Fire bowl that turns into a plancha, illustrative image. Höfats brand, Corten steel*

**Höfats** is a German design **brand** specialising in hearths and fire bowls. Its Bowl in **Corten steel** turns into a plancha and a barbecue thanks to accessories. With a price of 300 to 600 euros, it is the most affordable brand in the ranking, ideal for small spaces and tight budgets, even if the cooking surface stays small.

**Pros**

- Award-winning design brand, compact format.
- Lowest entry price in the comparison.
- Corten steel and modular system by accessories.

**Cons**

- Smaller cooking surface, less suited to large tables.
- Versatility dependent on accessories bought separately.

## TOP 4 = Feuerring, the Swiss design brand

![Feuerring, Corten steel cooking ring, Swiss brand](/images/blog/feuerring-brasero-plancha.webp)
*The Feuerring, a design cooking ring made in Switzerland. Photo Feuerring via feuerring.ch*

**Feuerring** is a Swiss **brand** that has become iconic with its steel cooking ring around a central hearth. Available in **Corten steel** or stainless steel, it champions handcrafted **manufacturing** and a sculptural aesthetic. It is the most high-end brand in the ranking, from 2,000 to 3,500 euros, for those seeking a design object as much as a cooking tool.

**Pros**

- Iconic design brand, Swiss handcrafted manufacturing.
- Corten or stainless steel, high finish quality.
- High durability.

**Cons**

- Highest price in the comparison.
- Plancha only, with no integrated barbecue-by-grate function.

## TOP 5 = OFYR, the premium Dutch brand

![OFYR Classic 100, Corten steel plancha brazier, Dutch brand](/images/blog/ofyr-classic-brasero-plancha.webp)
*The OFYR Classic 100, a premium Corten steel plancha. Photo OFYR via ofyr.com*

**OFYR** is the Dutch reference **brand** for the premium **plancha brazier**, with its roughly 100 cm plancha in **Corten steel** and a flawless finish. Its ecosystem of accessories and furniture is wide, but the **brand** stays on a plancha-around-a-hearth logic, with no barbecue function or integrated storage, at a price of 1,600 to 2,000 euros.

**Pros**

- Recognised premium brand, high-end finish.
- Large plancha in Corten steel.
- Wide ecosystem of accessories and furniture.

**Cons**

- High price, above COEO at a comparable diameter.
- Plancha only, barbecue accessories optional.

## How to choose the right plancha brazier brand

Choosing among the best **plancha brazier** brands means weighing four criteria: the country of **manufacturing** (French with COEO and Le Marquier, European with Höfats, Feuerring and OFYR), the number of cooking functions, the material and thickness of the **steel plancha**, and the budget. **Corten steel** dominates the top of the market for its **durability** with no paint maintenance.
