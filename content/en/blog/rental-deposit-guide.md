> **In brief:**
> 1. The security deposit is capped at 1 month rent excluding charges for unfurnished, 2 months for furnished (France).
> 2. Return must occur within 1 to 2 months after key handover, depending on move-out inspection.
> 3. Visale is a free state guarantee accessible to most tenants under 31 in France.
> 4. A delay in return triggers a 10 percent monthly penalty per month overdue.


## Deposit and guarantee: don't confuse them

The **guarantor** (caution) is a person (natural or legal) who commits to paying rent in case of tenant default. The **security deposit** is the amount paid by the tenant to the landlord to cover possible unpaid rent or damage.

### Legal caps

| Rental type | Security deposit cap |
|--------------------|-----------------------------|
| Unfurnished rental | 1 month rent excluding charges |
| Furnished rental | 2 months rent excluding charges |
| Mobility lease | None (prohibited) |

## Possible guarantees

Several schemes reassure landlords:

- **Joint guarantor** (caution solidaire): a family member commits in writing
- **Visale**: free state guarantee (Action Logement) for young and precarious workers
- **Unpaid Rent Guarantee** (GLI): insurance taken by the landlord
- **Bank guarantee**: the bank blocks a sum equivalent to several months of rent

## Return of the security deposit

> "The landlord has one month to return the deposit if the move-out inspection matches move-in, or two months if deductions are made. Beyond that, a 10 percent monthly penalty applies."
> — ALUR Law, article 22

### Authorized deductions

The landlord can deduct from the deposit:

- Unpaid rent and charges (justified)
- Unperformed rental repairs (quotes or invoices)
- Damages noted at move-out inspection
- Charges adjustment (with receipts)

## Visale: the free guarantee

Visale is accessible to all tenants under 31 in France, regardless of status. Beyond that, it targets salaried workers in precarious contracts, apprentices, scholarship students and households in professional mobility.

### Steps to subscribe to Visale

1. Create an account on visale.fr
2. Obtain the visa (before or during home search)
3. Present the visa to the landlord who validates
4. Sign the lease with Visale guarantee activated

## Frequently asked questions

<details>
<summary>What is the maximum security deposit in 2026?</summary>

The security deposit is legally capped at 1 month rent excluding charges for unfurnished rental and 2 months for furnished rental in France. For a mobility lease, no deposit is allowed. These caps are set by the 6 July 1989 law and the ALUR law.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How long to get back the security deposit?</summary>

The landlord has one month to return the deposit if move-out matches move-in inspection, or two months if deductions are made. Beyond that, the tenant can claim a 10 percent monthly penalty per started overdue month.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How to get the Visale guarantee?</summary>

Visale is accessible to all tenants under 31 in France, salaried workers in precarious contracts and scholarship students. Create an account on visale.fr, get the visa (valid 3 years) and present it to the landlord at lease signing. The guarantee is free and covers unpaid rent for 36 months.

</details>
