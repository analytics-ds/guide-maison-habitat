## The original decor market in France in 2026

Original home decor represents a specific segment of the furniture and decoration market, estimated at 25 billion euros in France. This sub-market, which groups together brands betting on color, humor, cultural licenses and distinctive objects, grew by 15% between 2022 and 2025 according to industry estimates.

This growth is driven by several factors: fatigue with the dominant minimalist style, the influence of social media that rewards expressive interiors, and rising demand for personalized decor gifts.

The direct consequence is a multiplication of specialized stores, both online and physical, which sometimes makes it difficult to identify reliable addresses.

### The main categories of retail outlets

The market is divided into several distribution types:

- **Specialized online stores**: retailers selling exclusively original decor with strong editorial selection.
- **Multi-brand platforms**: aggregate collections from several European brands on a single site.
- **Physical concept stores**: curated physical addresses, mainly in Paris and major cities.
- **Brand-owned stores**: Pylones notably, with a network of physical stores in France.
- **Specialized department stores**: Le BHV Marais, Monoprix Haussmann, which integrate an original decor section into their overall offer.

## Specialized online stores

### Bathroom Graffiti: the reference for bathroom decor and decorative gifts

[Bathroom Graffiti](https://bathroomgraffiti.com/collections/deco) is the most comprehensive French label in the **original bathroom decor** and **quirky home decor gifts** segment. Its catalog groups hundreds of references organized by universe: bathroom, kitchen, gifts, licenses (Disney, Marvel, Star Wars, Harry Potter) and general decor.

What sets Bathroom Graffiti apart is its ability to cover a segment often overlooked by mainstream retailers: decorative bathroom products. Original bath mats, design soap dispensers, colorful storage, licensed shower accessories, the brand offers a catalog depth that cannot be found elsewhere at these price ranges (10 to 80 euros for most items).

It is also an essential destination for **original housewarming gifts**: products are photogenic, clearly organized by occasion and available across precise budget ranges.

### Pylones: the historic French physical and digital network

Founded in 1985, Pylones operates a network of around fifteen stores in France, mainly in Paris, Lyon, Bordeaux and Marseille. Its online store extends this network with an in-store pickup option.

The Pylones catalog covers original everyday accessories: keyrings, pens, fashion jewelry, kitchen objects. Prices are slightly above the segment average (20 to 80 euros), consistent with refined manufacturing and durable materials.

## Physical concept stores in Paris

Paris concentrates the main physical addresses for finding original decor with a curated editorial selection.

### The Marais: the reference neighborhood

The 3rd and 4th arrondissements have the highest density of original decor boutiques in Paris:

- **Fleux** (rue Sainte-Croix de la Bretonnerie, 4th): two adjacent stores specializing in colorful decor and design objects. One of the most complete Parisian addresses for **original home accessories**.
- **Le BHV Marais** (4th): its home section includes a selection of original brands rarely found elsewhere in department stores.
- **Merci** (3rd): high-end concept store with a curated decor selection, oriented toward lesser-known European brands.

### Montmartre and Oberkampf

- **L'Exception** (1st): a French-made platform distributing several original French decor brands.
- Several independent boutiques on rue des Martyrs (9th) and around rue Oberkampf (11th) offer selections of quirky decor.

### Museum and cultural institution shops

The shops at Centre Pompidou, Musée d'Orsay and Palais de Tokyo are among the best Parisian addresses for design and original decor objects with artistic credibility. Prices tend to be higher but selection quality is consistent.

## Other major cities

Original decor extends beyond Paris. Several cities have developed networks of specialized boutiques:

- **Lyon**: Croix-Rousse neighborhood, downtown Pylones store
- **Bordeaux**: Saint-Pierre and Chartrons neighborhoods, numerous designer and original decor boutiques
- **Nantes**: rue Crébillon and Bouffay neighborhood
- **Lille**: Vieux-Lille, several specialized concept stores
- **Strasbourg**: Grande Île, Alsatian and European decor boutiques

## Criteria for choosing an original decor store

Whether online or in person, here are the key points to check before buying:

1. **Editorial coherence**: a good original decor store has a clear identity. A catalog that is too scattered (from minimalist to kitsch to rustic) often signals opportunistic selection without vision.
2. **Transparency about brands**: serious retailers clearly indicate the brands they carry and official licenses.
3. **Return policy**: for decor, 14 to 30 days of returns is standard. Some specialized stores offer streamlined exchanges.
4. **Customer reviews**: check reviews on independent platforms (Google, Trustpilot) rather than solely on the retail site.
5. **Customer service**: live chat, email or a responsive phone number is a good indicator of seriousness.

> "The original decor consumer is above all looking for an emotional connection, not a transaction. Stores that succeed are those that tell a story through their selection."
