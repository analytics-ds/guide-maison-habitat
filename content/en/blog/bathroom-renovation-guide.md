> **In brief:**
> 1. The average budget for a 5 to 8 sqm bathroom is between 5000 and 15000 euros.
> 2. Labor represents about 50 percent of the total project budget.
> 3. Relocating water inlets can cost up to 2500 euros: limit these changes if the budget is tight.
> 4. A complete project takes on average 2 to 3 weeks including drying.


## Bathroom renovation: key steps

**Bathroom renovation** is one of the most technical projects in a home. Plumbing, electricity, waterproofing, ventilation: there are many points of vigilance.

### Defining needs

Before requesting a quote, put your priorities on paper: shower or bathtub, double vanity, storage, laundry area. Trade-offs are often made according to available surface.

## Typical budget

| Item | Budget share | Range (6 sqm bathroom) |
|-------|------------------|----------------------------------|
| Labor | 50 percent | 2500 to 7500 euros |
| Tiling | 15 percent | 750 to 2250 euros |
| Sanitary | 15 percent | 750 to 2250 euros |
| Vanity unit | 10 percent | 500 to 1500 euros |
| Electricity, plumbing | 10 percent | 500 to 1500 euros |

## Pitfalls to avoid

> "The first pitfall is underestimating waterproofing. A poorly waterproofed bathroom deteriorates in 2 to 3 years."
> — French Building Federation

Always have the liquid waterproofing system (SEL) under tiling checked by a professional. This item does not show but determines the project's lifespan.

## Project steps

1. Demolition and removal of old equipment
2. Plumbing and electrical rework
3. Flooring and partition installation
4. Waterproofing application under tiling
5. Wall and floor tiling
6. Painting and finishes
7. Sanitary and vanity installation
8. Final connections and tests

## Available aid

For a bathroom adapted for elderly or mobility-impaired persons, MaPrimeAdapt' can fund up to 70 percent of the works. 10 percent VAT applies to labor for homes over 2 years old.

## Frequently asked questions

<details>
<summary>How much does a bathroom renovation cost?</summary>

The average budget for a complete bathroom renovation is between 5000 and 15000 euros for a 5 to 8 sqm room. A light renovation (refresh, vanity change) is around 3000 euros, against 15000 to 25000 euros for a high-end renovation with relocated plumbing.

</details>

<details>
<summary>How long does a bathroom renovation take?</summary>

A complete renovation takes on average 2 to 3 weeks: 3 days of demolition, 1 week of plumbing and electricity, 1 week of tiling and painting, then equipment installation. Add 1 to 2 weeks for finishes and drying.

</details>

<details>
<summary>Do I need a permit to renovate a bathroom?</summary>

For a renovation without modification of the external appearance and without creating surface, no authorization is generally required. In a condominium, check the rules: some works (plumbing, drain columns) require union authorization.

</details>
