## Who we are

Guide Maison & Habitat is an independent media dedicated to real estate and home. We publish weekly articles, comparisons and rankings to help owners, buyers and decor enthusiasts make the right choices.

## Our mission

Make quality information accessible on all home topics: buying, renovation, interior design, decoration, garden, financial aids. Our content is factual, sourced and regularly updated.

## Our editorial line

- Verified and sourced information
- No disguised sponsored content
- Transparency on affiliate links (when applicable)
- Regular updates of articles

## Our team

A team of passionate writers covers each field with expertise: interior designers, specialized journalists, craftsmen and real estate experts.
